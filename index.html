Design

I design interfaces and products that are simple, feasible and solve a problem before thinking about making it pretty. This is how I work:

  1. Draft: Understand what users need. Wireframes, sketches, mockups. Follow Occam’s Razor.
  2. Share: Good design is for everyone. Use InVision / Dropbox to iterate and gather feedback.
  3. Explore: Ideas are never complete. Work on some radical redesigns of the product for fun.
