My design process

I work as a web developer so my design workflow differs from that of a graphic designer or illustrator. I like to design things that are outside the box as it provides a challenge to craft them into reality. This is basically how I work:

1. Research

Design is not art - it’s a solution to a problem. It requires understanding what your users need. As Occam’s Razor states: the simplest solution is usually the correct one.

2. Mockup

Produce basic wireframes, sketches and mockups in my DotGrid book. Pen and paper is still my favourite way of jotting down an idea that needs further refinement.

3. Share

Good design doesn’t belong to you, it belongs to the world. I use Invision and Dropbox to share my ideas with others, gather feedback and keep iterating until it works.

4. Explore

Ideas are never complete as long as someone is working on them. After completing a design, I like to work on radical designs that re-imagine the core concept of the product.
